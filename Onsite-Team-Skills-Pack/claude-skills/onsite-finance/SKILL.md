# Onsite Finance Context

## Revenue Model

**Attribution:** Always use `sale_done_date` for revenue. Parse `annual_revenue` (strip "Rs."); never use `Revenue` (score).

**Deal size (ref):** India avg ~Rs.52K, ME ~Rs.1.04L. Website ~Rs.76K, Google AD ~Rs.88K, Support WA ~Rs.40K.

**Trend:** Revenue flat Rs.25–35L/month. ~42–74 sales/month, avg deal Rs.40–76K. No growth trajectory in recent 12 months.

---

## Benchmarks

- LTV:CAC 3:1 min. CAC payback <12 mo. Monthly churn <3.5%. Demo→close 20–30% healthy; Onsite 14% (improve).
- Sales cycle construction avg 147 days; Onsite ~47 days.

---

## Risk

- 50–70% revenue from organic/direct (no campaign attribution). ME 10–20% of total. Diversify paid and ME.

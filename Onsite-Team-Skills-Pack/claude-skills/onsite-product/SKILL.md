# Onsite Product & Feature Context

## Feature Priority (Construction SaaS)

| Priority | Feature | Why |
|---------|---------|-----|
| 1 | Labour attendance & tracking | 20–40% budget, #1 leakage |
| 2 | Real-time site progress / daily reports | Site–office info lag |
| 3 | Payroll integration | Overtime, compliance, India-critical |
| 4 | Document management | Outdated plans, rework |
| 5 | Subcontractor management | Work orders, payments |
| 6 | Material tracking & inventory | Wastage, theft |
| 7 | Safety & compliance | Regulatory, ME-critical |
| 8 | Financial / cost tracking (ERP) | Budget vs actuals |
| 9 | Scheduling & task management | Cross-project resources |
| 10 | Analytics & reporting | Executive visibility |

India focus: Labour, daily reports, payment tracking. ME: BIM, safety, multi-project, mega-project visibility.

---

## Product Metrics (Targets)

- DAU/MAU (stickiness) 20%+. Activation 65%+. Feature adoption 30%+ per core feature. Time to value <10 min. 7-day retention 80%+, 30-day 60%+. NPS 40+ good, 50+ excellent. Trial→Paid 20–25% (no CC), 49–60% (CC required).

---

## Mobile Imperatives

- Offline-first (poor site connectivity). Android-first (India + ME). Simple UI (gloves, sunlight). Low data usage. Quick actions (attendance in 2 taps, photo in 1).

---

## Analysis Patterns

- **Feature→revenue:** Map ad_name to feature category → sales from that ad.
- **PMF signals:** High website conv, short Google AD cycle = strong; low demo→sale, high NATC = weak.
- **Churn risk:** Trial→purchased conversion, "Not Interested (P)" after demo, support tickets by feature.
